In case your test suite needs access to sensitive configuration (passwords, API tokens etc.) you can store them in environment variables. To configure environment variables for your test suite on Semaphore, follow the steps bellow.

1. Click on the little cog next to your project name on dashboard

!/assets/images/envvar-dashboard.png!

2. Follow "Set up Environment Variables" on project settings page

!/assets/images/envvar-project-settings.png!

3. Add your variables through the form bellow

!/assets/images/envvar-setup.png!
